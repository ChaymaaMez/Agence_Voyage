Our rooms

Each room, with a different Moroccan style, envelops you in the softness of the tadelakt of its walls and allows you to admire some original works of art.
The junior suites consist of a bedroom extended by a comfortably furnished living area.
All the rooms are equipped with a bathroom with a "rainwater" shower, independent toilets, reversible air conditioning, free Internet connection (WIFI), a 16: 9 satellite television, a radio / music sound system, an individual safe. Very comfortable bedding, Egyptian cotton sheets, hospitality products, hair dryer.
The rooms are non-smoking. Pets are not accepted.
The copious Moroccan breakfast is offered to you.
